This document defines the three types of levels for sending client details.

Levels and their details:
    1. Level 1:
        This level is for basic details such as:
            a. Name
            b. Profile-Image
    2. Level 2:
        This is for medium level details such as:
            a. Level 1 details
            b. Phone Number
            c. Email
    3. Level 3:
        This is the Admin-Level, which is the highest level of details, exposes all the details such as:
            a. Level 2 details
            b. Created At
            c. List of Jobs posted
            d. Local Currency

- Access Permission for levels:
    1. Level 1:
        a. When Job is (Posted, In-Deal & Closed) and Worker needs some details
    2. Level 2:
        a. When Job is in (Assigned & Being Worked Currently) and worker needs details
    3. Level 3:
        a. When Client needs their details
        b. When Dashboard Admin Needs any client details
